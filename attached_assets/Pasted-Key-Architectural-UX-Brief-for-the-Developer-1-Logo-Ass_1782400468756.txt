Key Architectural & UX Brief for the Developer
1. Logo Assessment (Addressing your specific question)
Verdict: Do not change the geometric sans-serif wordmark ("AuraCloset" in Inter Bold), but completely invert its optical hierarchy.

Why the current execution fails: In your screenshots, AuraCloset is rendered in dark navy #101826 against flat grey #F5F3F0. This looks like a standard tax-filing or utility dashboard.
Why the wordmark itself works: Pure typography without illustrative clip-art (like clothes hangers or closet doors) is the universal standard for ultra-luxury fashion houses (Celine, Saint Laurent, Bottega Veneta) and premium tech (Apple, Tesla).
The Developer Prescription:
Invert the wordmark color to Crisp Off-White (#FFFFFF) with a delicate dispersion shadow.
Directly above AuraCloset, inject an uppercase micro-header: A U R A C L O S E T A T E L I E R (Inter Medium, 10pt, Champagne Gold #D0B892, wide tracking +4.5px). This tension between micro-tracking and bold headlines immediately screams high fashion.
2. Screen 1 (Landing) & Screen 2 (Auth) Background Continuity
The "No-Reload" Contract: When transitioning from Screen 1 (Get started) to Screen 2 (Sign-in), the background photograph closet.jpg must not reload, cut, or reset.
Living Motion: Animate the photograph with a continuous 16-second looping Ken Burns zoom (scale: 1.0 -> 1.08) using React Native Reanimated.
Dynamic Focus Scrim: On Screen 1, a 3-stop LinearGradient overlay (35% top to 96% navy bottom) keeps the warm ceiling recessed lights visible. As Screen 2 transitions in, increase the mid-screen scrim opacity from 0.65 to 0.92 (or mount a secondary BlurView). This creates optical depth of field, locking the user's eye directly onto the input fields.
3. Frosted Glassmorphism Inputs (BlurView)
Replaces flat white boxes with Translucent Frosted Glass Inputs (backgroundColor: rgba(255,255,255,0.06), hairline 1px border rgba(255,255,255,0.14)).
Tactile Focus State: When an input receives focus, animate the border color to Champagne Gold (#D0B892) with a subtle 3px outer glow.
4. Animation Choreography Matrix (Mandatory <300ms)
Conforming strictly to §12 of TECHNICAL.md:

Entering Stagger: Screen elements mount via FadeInDown.duration(280) with a silky 60ms progressive layer stagger (Brand -> Social Pills -> Tab Bar -> Inputs -> Submit CTA).
Tactile Haptics: Tapping any button triggers Haptics.impactAsync(Light) while Reanimated spring physics momentarily depress the glass container to scale: 0.97.
